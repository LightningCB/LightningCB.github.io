WELCOME TO MY WEBSITE!

I'm Cole--an aspiring Screenwriter, Narrative Designer, and Comic-Book Author, passionate about the art of storytelling in all of its forms.

It took me a while to fully commit to pursuing professional storytelling. Over the years, I've studied and trained in public policy, public relations, legislative advocacy, architecture, psychology, songwriting, animation, philosophy--to save you time (and save me website space), I'll stop there. It took almost two years of college and a surprise visit from Scott Budnick to help me realize that what drew me to each of these fields was their ability to understand, create, and share feelings and experiences--to create empathy. I transferred into the creative writing department that night, and never looked back. Since then, I've become a much better storyteller, but my fixation has remained consistent. I specialize in creating narratives that allow the audience to feel the emotions and experiences on a page--or screen--as if they were their own.

Please feel free to take a look around! A lot of work went into this website--I designed it by hand! My work samples can be found below, or through the hamburger menu on the top right of the header. I can't wait to hear from you!

Yours truly,

Cole
